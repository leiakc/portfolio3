Leia Kook-Chun is currently exploring her creative practice, dipping in various jobs and creative media to see how she can best make an impact on the world.

By studying art & technology at OCAD University’s Digital Futures program, her mind is open to the possibilities as she learns new methods for creative work and thinking.

Her professional experience includes leading a workshop for the OCAD Student Union, participating in a panel discussion with FeelSpace Curatorial about art in digital space, working as a product photographer for Trudelle Laker Jewellery, selling her handmade jewellery at the OCAD Artist Alley, and exhibiting her artwork at various exhibits.

During her personal time, Leia experiments with artistic media to express herself. She creates artwork to process her emotional turmoil and shares it with the world so people can feel seen, understood, and connected. Art can be healing, enlightening, inspiring, and so much more. Art can be anything.
