2021

This project explores the behaviour economy in relation to our technological world and data as a material.

Key notes about technology include:

  • Accessibility is easier than ever – technology has made it easy to access and do anything
  • Information & technology is neutral – how it’s used depends on individuals
  • Technology is evolving at an exponential rate

Key notes about behaviour include:
  • Positive and negative aspects of human nature are brought to the forefront online
  • Self-discipline becomes a skill that needs to be developed with increased ease of access
  • Intimate and taboo content can deepen an individual’s relationship with themselves
  • Human behaviour is changing with the use of technology and the online world
  • Heightened awareness of self comes with the self-presentation online

Key notes about effects include:
  • Digital world is part of our reality
  • Everything is augmented and emphasized
  • New danger and safety
  • New societal pressures
  • More power to the people
