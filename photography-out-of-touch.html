Photomanipulation with Photoshop • 2021

As we lock ourselves away by staying inside during the pandemic, we grow to feel disconnected, caged, and drained. Here, nature (depicted on the right) reaches from outside, which is surrounded by light, to uplift the hand on the left, which is surrounded by darkness. The light symbolically represents positivity and inspiration, while the darkness represents the tension and stress that contribute to an unhealthy state of mind.

We are out of touch with nature, so we need to remember to explore the great outdoors and connect with the earth. It’s healthy and inspiring to go outside – nature is good for one’s mental health.

This piece references Michelangelo’s work “The Creation of Adam” with the hand gestures and positions. In “The Creation of Adam,” it’s said that the anticipated contact between the two fingers breathes life into Adam (the man on the left) from the Lord (the man on the right.) “Out of Touch” compares nature to the Lord (inspiring, giving light, and improving one’s well-being) and Adam to an unhealthy state of mind.

For the hand that symbolises nature, I chose the texture of moss and leaves – moss brings a softness that is comforting. The idea of nature being comforting supports the concept of nature promoting wellbeing. The details in the texture are alluring, like that of an artwork; this contributes to the idea that nature is art, and we can find healing through appreciating and absorbing the art around us.
