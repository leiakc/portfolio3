Acrylic on Wood • 2cm x 5cm • 2019

With its expressive brushwork, colour, and composition, these earrings are a reminder to speak from the heart – that is where you’ll find your truest expression of yourself.

To make this set, wooden canvas keys (small wooden wedges that come with canvas) were repurposed and adapted to be worn as earrings.
