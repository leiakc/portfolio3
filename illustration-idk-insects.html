Mixed Media: Ink on Paper & Acrylic on Canvas • 2020

IDK Insects explores self-doubt, existentialism, and a sense of lack of control in one’s life. We all have these negative thoughts from time to time – this art series aims to make people feel seen and understood in our shared experience of these thoughts. It’s relatabeetle.

Existential Butterfly

“WHY DO I EXIST” is written on a butterfly’s wings.

This illustration was inspired by thoughts like:

  • What is my purpose in life?
  • What is existence?
  • Is there a reason I am here?

What Am I Doing Beetle

“WHAT AM I DOING” appears on a beetle’s back.

This piece was inspired by feelings of uncertainty and self-doubt when doing something.

How Do I Survive Moth

“HOW DO I SURVIVE” goes across a moth’s wingspan.

This drawing was inspired by questions surrounding survival:

  • How will I be able to make enough money to survive?
  • How has survival changed for people overtime?
  • How is survival different for animals?
  • What do you need to survive?
